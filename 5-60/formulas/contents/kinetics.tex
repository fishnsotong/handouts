\section{Kinetics of Chemical Reactions}

\subsection*{Arrhenius Theory}

\begin{equation*}
    k = Ae^{-E_a/RT}
\end{equation*}

Small $E_a$ $\rightarrow$ weak $T$-dependence $\rightarrow$ fast \\
Large $E_a$ $\rightarrow$ strong $T$-dependence $\rightarrow$ slow \\
