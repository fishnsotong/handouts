
\section{Probability Models and Axioms}

\subsection*{Probability Axioms}
Nonnegativity: $P(A) \geq 0$

Normalisation: $P(\Omega) = 1$

Finite additivity: If $A \cap B = \emptyset$, then 

$P(A \cup B) = P(A) + P(B)$.

\subsection*{Venn Diagrams}
